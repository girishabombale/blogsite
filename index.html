About Me

Dr. Girisha Ramhari Bombale is a dedicated and experienced academician and software professional with a strong background in Computer Engineering. She is currently serving as the Head of Department & Assistant Professor at SND College of Engineering & Research Center, Babhulgaon, Yeola (Nashik).

With a Ph.D. in Computer Science Engineering awarded in 2024, Dr. Bombale has a profound understanding of various computer science domains including Machine Learning, Data Modelling and Visualization, Cyber Security, and Object-Oriented Programming. Her research interests include Mobile Cellular Telecommunication, Network Security, BREW, and SWARM Robotics.

She is a Life Member of the Indian Society of Technical Education (LMISTE), Society for Data Science (S4DS), and International Association of Engineering (IAENG), actively contributing to the academic and research community.
